Where I've built things

Dec 2021 – Present
Analytics Engineer · formerly Senior Data Analyst
WORK180 — HR Tech & Employer Endorsement Platform

Built and own the entire data infrastructure for WORK180 — from streaming pipelines through to self-service reporting — serving every team in the organisation. Sole owner of the data function end-to-end: pipeline architecture, data modelling, transformation logic, and reporting.

Designed and built the data pipeline from scratch using KSQL, streaming and transforming data from CRM, product event logs, website behaviour, DEI assessments, and portal usage into a structured warehouse of 76 tables. Own all transformation logic from raw source to reporting-ready layer.

Beyond the infrastructure, I've built tools that directly changed how the business operates. One I'm particularly proud of: an employer benchmarking report I created entirely on my own initiative — no one asked for it. It compares endorsed vs non-endorsed employers across key gender equity metrics, giving sales and renewals teams concrete, data-backed evidence of the real-world impact of WORK180 endorsement. It became a core commercial tool used in pitches and renewals.

I also designed and built a client health monitor scoring every client across 30 engagement and performance metrics to forecast renewal likelihood and surface churn risk early — and an interactive ROI calculator for enterprise sales with live industry benchmarking and multi-year financial modelling.
